Marketash: Transforming Agriculture Through Technology

Introduction: Marketash is a tech-driven platform revolutionizing agribusiness by connecting farmers directly with buyers and empowering them with AI-powered tools.

Unique Value Proposition: AI-powered farm consultation, direct-to-buyer sales, bidding systems, financial inclusion, and data-driven insights.

Target Market: Small-scale farmers, buyers, retailers, NGOs, and policymakers.

Revenue Model: Transaction fees, premium AI consultation, subscription plans, data analytics services, and advertisements.

Marketing Strategy Highlights:

  • Storytelling and farmer success stories for community engagement.
  • Partnerships with influencers, NGOs, and institutions.
  • Community outreach and referral incentive programs.
